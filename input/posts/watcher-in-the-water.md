Title: Lord of the Rings LCG - The Watcher in the Water
Published: 03/31/2019
Tags:
   - Lord of the Rings
   - Living Card Game
---
The Watcher in the Water is the 3rd Adventure Pack from the Dwarrowdelf Cycle. It is also known as the "Aragorn" adventure pack, as it features a version of [Aragorn](http://hallofbeorn.com/LotR/Details/Aragorn-TWitW?Lang=EN) from the Lore sphere which is one of the best heroes in the game. You also get his sword Narsil, though it is called [Sword That Was Broken](http://hallofbeorn.com/LotR/Details/Sword-that-was-Broken-TWitW?Lang=EN) in the game. You also get the ally version of [Arwen](http://hallofbeorn.com/LotR/Details/Arwen-Undomiel-TWitW?Lang=EN) which is a very good card as well.

The quest is reminiscent of the scene in the Fellowship of the Ring movie, where the Fellowship was trying to find a way to enter Moria. There are two ways to beat this quest: you have to be able to solve the riddle from the Doors of Durin OR you have to defeat The Watcher. 

To clear the Doors of Durin, you need some way to know what the top card on the encounter deck is. Other than [Henamarth Riversong](http://hallofbeorn.com/LotR/Details/Henamarth-Riversong-Core?Lang=EN), I had no other way to "scry" the encounter deck, so I didn't really have a choice, I needed to defeat The Watcher. Initially I thought I could just hold off the enemies while I wait for Henamarth to show up, however when the quest progressed and The Watcher showed up, I was forced to find a way to defeat The Watcher. 

It wasn't easy, but the trio of [Aragorn](http://hallofbeorn.com/LotR/Details/Aragorn-TWitW?Lang=EN), [Frodo](http://hallofbeorn.com/LotR/Details/Frodo-Baggins-CatC?Lang=EN) and [Glorfindel](http://hallofbeorn.com/LotR/Details/Glorfindel-FoS?Lang=EN) turns out to be a very effective group. Frodo with his ability to absorb damage as a threat increase, makes him a superb blocker. It also allows you to take an undefended attack, wherein you just assign the damage to Frodo and it will be convered to a threat increase. This really synergizes well with Aragorn's ability to reset the threat level later in the game. Add Glorfindel's really low threat level and you start the game with a threat level of only 24; that is pretty good! With Frodo taking defending duties, this freed up other heroes and allies to pile on the attack on The Watcher. Gandalf also showed up just in time to help the group finish off the Watcher.
